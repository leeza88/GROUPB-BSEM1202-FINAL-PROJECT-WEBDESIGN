Our Mission

Healthcare for Every Sierra Leonean

Founded with a deep passion for improving health outcomes in Sierra Leone, SaloneMedConnect was created to address one of the most pressing challenges in our healthcare system — accessibility. Millions of Sierra Leoneans face barriers to accessing quality medical care due to distance, limited information, and lack of connectivity between patients and providers.

We built SaloneMedConnect to eliminate these barriers. Through our digital platform, patients from Freetown to Kailahun can find verified doctors, book appointments, access health information, and take control of their health journey.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Accessible Healthcare

We believe healthcare is a right, not a privilege. We work to remove every barrier that prevents Sierra Leoneans from accessing medical care.

Trusted Professionals

Every doctor on our platform is verified by the Sierra Leone Medical and Dental Council (SLMDC) and committed to excellence.

Digital Innovation

We harness technology to bring world-class digital health solutions tailored to Sierra Leone's unique healthcare needs and context.

Patient-Centred Care

Every decision we make starts with one question: How does this improve the patient's experience and health outcome?

Our Journey

Building Better Healthcare Together

2020

The Idea is Born

SaloneMedConnect was conceived during the COVID-19 pandemic when the need for digital health access in Sierra Leone became undeniable.

2021

Research & Development

A dedicated team of Sierra Leonean healthcare professionals, developers, and public health experts began building the platform.

2022

Platform Launch

SaloneMedConnect officially launched in Freetown with 25 verified doctors and a mission to serve all 14 districts of Sierra Leone.

2023

Nationwide Expansion

We expanded to Bo, Kenema, Makeni, and Koidu — bringing our network to over 100 verified healthcare professionals.

2024–Present

Growing Stronger

With 150+ doctors, 5,000+ patients served, and 40+ partner hospitals, we are just getting started on our mission.
