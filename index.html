About SaloneMedConnect

Transforming Healthcare Access Across Sierra Leone

SaloneMedConnect is Sierra Leone's leading digital health platform, dedicated to bridging the gap between patients and qualified healthcare providers. We connect people in Freetown, Bo, Kenema, Makeni, and beyond to trusted doctors, hospitals, and clinics — all through a simple, accessible digital experience.

Our mission is to make quality healthcare accessible to every Sierra Leonean regardless of location, empowering communities with the tools and information they need to lead healthier lives.
